Interpreting medical images such as radiology images can be a time-consuming task that requires the expertise of professionals in the field, and ends up causing delays in the clinical diagnosis pipeline. Therefore, there is the need of having automatic methods that can approximately detect visual information and transform it into textual descriptions, in this case, medical concepts.

This websites has the purpose to present the work submitted by the NLIP-Essex-ITESM team during ImageCLEFmed concept detection 2021 sub-task. It is worth mentioning that the following system got ranked 6th, and NLIP-Essex-ITESM team got 2nd place.

This website was primarily build for learning how to adapt a research model and make it available for end-users. The predicted concepts must not be taken as the sole truth.
